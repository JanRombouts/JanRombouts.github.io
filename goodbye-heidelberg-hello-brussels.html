Goodbye Heidelberg, hello Brussels

  • Wed 01 January 2025
  • misc

After three years, I say goodbye to Heidelberg. My postdoc at EMBL comes to an end but the project and collaborations will go on. It was a very inspiring time and I will be happy to visit again once in a while!

I am now starting a new position at the Université Libre de Bruxelles with Geneviève Dupont in the Unit of Theoretical Chronobiology. I will be working as an FNRS-funded postdoc (chargé de recherches), continuing my research on pattern formation in developmental biology.
