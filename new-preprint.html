New preprint.

  • Mon 22 September 2025
  • misc

The first preprint from my postdoctoral work, in collaboration with Michael Zhao, Anna Erzberger and Alexander Aulehla is now out! In it, we use nonlocal advection-diffusion models to study aggregation-based patterning. In particular, we ask how the size of a confining domain determines patterning dynamics. We find that whereas the long-time attracting state is always the same, the transient dynamics are highly variable dependent on the domain size. We also did some fun mathematics and predict the outcome of an exciting set of experiments by Michael. Find the preprint on arXiv!
