Final event with the Young Marsilius Fellows

  • Thu 18 July 2024
  • misc

Together with the Young Marsilius Fellows of the Marsilius Kolleg of Heidelberg University, we organized an event with discussions, talks, games and an exhibition with posters from our 'Data is Art' competition. The title was 'From end to Beginning: An Interdisciplinary Adventure'. We talked about our research and how we found unexpected connections between very different fields, we had discussions about the meaning of heritage and opened up the black box of progress in science.

It was a great afternoon and I think the participants enjoyed it a lot.

I contributed a computer game where players have to cooperate and communicate in order to accomplish a common goal (see also Outreach).
